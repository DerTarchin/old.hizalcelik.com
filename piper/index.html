    Piper of Hamelin

    2D puppet animation using After Effects.

    About

    This project, still a work in progess, was made for a digital media class assignment involving folk tales. My goal was to make an artistic, stylized animation that could tell the story of the Pied Piper of Hamelin without dialogue.

    Design Choices

    The art style is inspired by the dark and silent beauty of Limbo. The color of the glowing "music" particles and the musical melody and pace both represent the emotions of the character. The music itself was chosen for the panflute solos and medieval tones.
